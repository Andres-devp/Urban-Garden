Sustainable
Practices

Juan

100% Organic Agriculture

  • No chemical pesticides or industrial fertilizers
  • Protects food production and human health
Sonia

Organic Composting

  • Fruit peels, vegetable scraps, coffee grounds
  • Mixed with dry leaves to create natural fertilizer
Ana Maria Andres

Water Harvesting & Biodiversity

  • Plastic tanks collect rainwater from roofs
  • Healthy soil creates safe environment for pollinators
  • Seed conservation: protect native and free seeds

Section 5 — Critical Thinking

Challenges &
Suggestions

Juan

Challenges

  • Heavy air pollution from buses and cars can affect crops
  • Citizens live in small apartments — not enough space or time
  • Limited funding for maintenance and expansion
Ana Maria

Our Suggestions

  • Teach workshops about vertical gardens for small balconies
  • Use social media (TikTok, Instagram) to invite younger students
  • Create partnerships with schools and universities

"Urban gardens face challenges, but they represent hope for healthier, more connected communities."
